Datty Plays

My YouTube Channel

I have played piano pretty much my whole life, and it is one of my greatest passions. After completing my grade 8 certificate and my high school music course in 2015, I found that I was lacking motivation to continue learning these difficult pieces to a performance standard. I had no opportunities to perform these pieces that I had put in a lot of effort to learn, so what was the point?


I managed to find the perfect solution to this problem, which was to create my own YouTube channel - Datty Plays. This channel allows me to film my performances and share them with the world. The resulting product is something I am extremely proud of, and I am always looking forward to learning new pieces and performing them for the channel. On this page I have compiled a collection of all my videos. Enjoy.
